Welcome

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Ottawa, focusing on the numerical analysis of partial differential equations. My current research centers on developing advanced finite element methods for the Richards equation to predict water movement in porous media.

Beyond my academic research, I serve as a graduate mentor and Teaching Assistant, and I create mathematical animations to make complex concepts more accessible and engaging.
